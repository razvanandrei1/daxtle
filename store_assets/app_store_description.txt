Daxtle — Minimalist Puzzle

Slide colored blocks across elegant boards to match each one with its target. Simple to learn, deeply satisfying to master.

THINK, DON'T RUSH
Every block moves in one fixed direction. Every swipe moves all blocks of that direction together. The challenge isn't speed — it's figuring out the right order. One wrong move and you'll need to rethink everything.

PURE LOGIC, NO FILLER
• No timers in campaign — take all the time you need
• No lives, no fail state — reset instantly with a double-tap
• No ads interrupting your flow
• No tutorials longer than one sentence

MECHANICS THAT UNFOLD
What starts as sliding a single block grows into a rich puzzle language:
• Push chains — blocks shove each other into position
• Cargo blocks — they can't move alone, only when pushed
• Teleport portals — warp across split boards
• Destroy blocks — sacrifice the right piece to clear the path
• Multiple colors — each with its own targets and traps

20 HAND-CRAFTED CAMPAIGN LEVELS
Each one designed around a single "aha" moment. No filler levels. No padding. Every puzzle earns its place.

CHALLENGE MODE
Solve as many puzzles as you can in a row. The timer shrinks after each solve. No resets. No undo. One mistake and it's over. 73 challenge puzzles across three difficulty tiers.
